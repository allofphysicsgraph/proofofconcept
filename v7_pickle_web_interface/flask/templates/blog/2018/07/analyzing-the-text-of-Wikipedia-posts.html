analyzing the text of Wikipedia posts

Published 2018-07-21T01:59:00.002Z by Physics Derivation Graph

In a previous post, an outline for analyzing Wikipedia content was described. In this post, I document a few initial observations about the data collected from Wikipedia.

Searching for "derivation" as a section marker means searching for "=== Derivation ===". There are other meanings to derivation, so sometimes the results include non-mathematical content like "=== Derivation and other names ===". To filter out irrelevant content, only sections with mathematical expressions (ie ":&lt;math&gt;") are relevant.
