This forms one of the core databases for the Physics Derivation Graph.
The first column is the name of the derivation, the second is the derivation step index.
The third and fourth column are the "from" node -- temporary and permanent labels.
The fifth and sixth column are the "to" node -- temporary and permanent labels.

The permanent expression index points to the expressions_database.xml
The inference rules are plain tex.
